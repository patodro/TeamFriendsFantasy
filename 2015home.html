Background

League format for the 2015 season remained the same. After controversy amid Schwert's involvement in 2014, his team ownership was forfeited and taken over by Tyler Olivieri.

2015 was deemed a 'reset year', with randomized draft order and no keepers.

League dues were increased for the first time to $35. Weekly hi score prizes were also added at $7 per week.

From the annals of time an unearthed poem served as a warning to inclement weather in the fall of 2015.
