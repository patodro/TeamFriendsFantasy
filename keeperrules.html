Keeper Rules

Each team may keep up to 1 player. Kept players will replace that team’s draft pick according to the following formula:
Prev yr draft rd-(yrs kept)

A player’s keeper-tenure will follow them until they are released back into the draft pool.

No player may be kept if it would force a team to lose a 1st or 2nd round pick. ( Result of the above equation must be > 2).

Keeper-eligible players must be on a roster by the trade deadline (technically 11:59pm on the night before the trade deadline)

Free Agent players will be given the equivalent of a final round draft pick. Draft order will be reverse standings of previous season.
