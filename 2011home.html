Background

The 2011 season was the inagural season for the Team Friends Fantasy Football League.

There are no records of payments for this season, so it's likely there were no prizes or payouts.

This may have been several managers virgin attempts at the budding phenomenon that is Fantasy Football, judging by 5 QBs taken in the first round.

Draft

2011 Draft Date: ??

The 2011 TFFF Draft was an in-person draft, hosted at the Ziemianski household in West Seneca, NY.

The was probably a massive white board with every pick written down, and it's also likely that not everyone was in attendance.

Draft Notables

Peyton Manning, selected by Z at 2.8, missed this entire season with a neck injury. The #Hashtags would attempt to replace Manning with Kevin Kolb before riding the Cam Newton rookie train to the inagural championship.

Fisher and the Panda Paws drafted STL K Josh Brown at 5.4
