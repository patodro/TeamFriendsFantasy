Background

Tyler Olivieri retired from league ownership after the 2016 season. The vacant team was adopted by Andy Kearney for the 2017 season, Team Friends Fantasy Football League was now 17% Kearney.

League dues remained at $50 - However, 2 new payout categories were added - Most Points and Best Record (both Regular Season).

Draft

2017 Draft Date: Thu August 31, 2017 8:00pm

The 2017 Draft was the first Live Online draft for TFFF. This was likely due to the league's geographic spreading as well as busy lives of 26 year olds.

The 2017 draft order was randomized, no keepers were permitted.
