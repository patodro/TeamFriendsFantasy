Background

Following the end of the 2024 season, Boyce announced his retirement from Team Friends. After 13 seasons, Boyce was 1 of 4 managers to win multiple championships. He finished his career with a 0.420 win pct.

Steve joined Team Friends as Boyce's replacement in 2025.
