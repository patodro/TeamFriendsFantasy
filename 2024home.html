Background

A major innovation was introduced with the 2024 season, a custom-made trophy awarded to the champion each year. The commissioner invested league resources into new equipment for etching the winning team name on a shot glass. A decision that would prove fruitful for the commissioner that season.

Draft

2024 Online Draft Date: Sun September 03, 2024 08:00pm EDT

A pre-draft trade was accepted between Andy and Bobby. Andy received the 6th draft slot and pick 12.2 in exchange for the 11th draft slot and pick 4.7

Andy "Drew Rosenhaus" Kearney stunned the league by confirming a 2nd set of pick trades in as many days. Sending picks 6.7 and 7.6 to Sean for picks 5.4 and 8.9
