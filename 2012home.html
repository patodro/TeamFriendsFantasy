Background

The 2012 season saw a significant expansion for the Team Friends Fantasy Football League.

Managers held over from the 2011 season included Pat, Dave, Sean, and Z. New managers that lasted more than 1 season included Bobby, Tony, Boyce, Pfeifer, and Glaub

There was a $20 buy-in. Payouts were for top 3 playoff finishers.
