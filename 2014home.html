Background

Major changes came about for the 2014 season of Team Friends Fantasy Football. The league expanded to 12 teams, playoffs expanded to include 6 teams, and 2 divisions were formed.

Trevor and Cory divisions took their names from a pair of pushover idiots from the show Trailer Park Boys. A true time capsule to what was relevant to league managers at the time.

3 new managers became a part of the 2014 class of TFFF: Schwert, Lepo, and Josh.

Again, some semblance of keepers was put in play for the 2014 draft, albeit with nebulous motives and rules.

There was a $20 buy-in. Payouts were for top 3 playoff finishers.

Draft

2014 Draft Date: Sun August 24, 2014 9:00pm

The 2014 TFFF Draft was an in-person draft, likely hosted at Pat's new home @ Ashford Pl. in Depew, NY.

A rudimentary system of keepers was retained for the 2014 draft. Each team could keep 1 player from their previous season's roster. All keepers would be set during round 1 of the draft. It seems like the 2 new franchises were each awarded a 1st round pick to use in lieu of keepers?

Honestly, I'm not really sure how that makes sense, man.

It was also made known that the league would be entering a "reset year" meaning 2015's draft would not utilize keepers.
